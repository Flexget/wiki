Currently:

{{{
series:
  - some serie:
      timeframe:
        hours: 4
        enough: 720p
  - another serie
  - third serie
}}}

Timeframe indentation causes a lot of problems for new users, also the ''':''' in '''some serie'''.

Alternative implementation:

{{{
series:
  - name: some serie
    timeframe:
      hours: 4
      enough: 720p
  - another serie
  - third serie
}}}
