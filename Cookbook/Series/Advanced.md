= Advanced example =

How to use most of the series plugin features, with template:

{{{
templates:
  tv:
    series:
      settings:
        720p:
          timeframe: 12 hours

      720p:
        - house
        - chuck 
 
      normal:
        - the colbert report

    download: ~/watch/torrent/series/

feeds:
  my-feed-a:
    rss: http://example.com/feed-a.rss
    template: tv

  my-feed-b:
    rss: http://example.com/feed-b.rss
    template: tv

}}}
