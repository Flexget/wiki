= Nyaa =
Search plugin which gives results from [http://nyaa.se/ nyaa.se]
=== Config ===
Takes two options:

'''Category:'''

Valid choices are {{{all}}}, {{{anime}}}, {{{anime eng}}}, {{{anime non-eng}}}, and {{{anime raw}}}

'''Filter:'''

Valid choices are {{{all}}}, {{{filter remakes}}}, {{{trusted only}}}, and {{{a+ only}}}



=== Example ===
{{{
nyaa:
  category: anime eng
  filter: trusted only
}}}
